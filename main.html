Olivia Go Loves Italian Food

Bio

I’m just a girl from a small town in California. I like my family, my dog, my friends, of course… but I’m in love with Italian food. When I’m not eating Italian food, you can find me thinking, reading, or writing about Italian food. My name is Olivia Go and I. Love. Italian. Food.

Poetry

An Ode to Italian Food

Oh Italian food
Sweet Italian food
One bite fixes all my bad moods
Spaghetti, Pizza, Ravioli, and more
Just seeing you makes my heart soar!
Oh Italian food
Sweet Italian food
How I love you so!
You are so much better than any boy
I will never let you go

Favorite Italian Restaurants

Lamonica’s NY Pizza $:

A New York style pizzeria that sells super cheap calzones and pizza by the slice. They have the best sausage pizza in the world.

 Pizzaria Mozza $$:

A cute Italian restaurant that sells the most amazing pizza crust and their meatballs are sO good. Kinda pricey but the ingredients are so fresh so it's worth it.

Osteria Mozza $$$:

A really high-end Italian restaurant. The regulars are old rich people. One thing off the menu might cost a whole week of groceries but anything for super good Italian food amirite?
